Our History:

Our company was founded in Seattle in 1998 by our dearest friends Jacky and Milan Trykar.

They retired in 2017, giving my husband and me (Gustavo and Valerie Hernandez) the option to purchase the company. Growing up, we had always worked together in the hospitality industry, learning the standards of good cleaning and the importance of teamwork and quality customer service.

Thanks to Jacky and Milan, we were given the greatest blessing of our lives, which of course was taking over this great company. They taught us the foundations of their business and how to strive for success. We plan to give our clients the best-quality service, consistency, reliability, and loyalty. To us, our clients are and always will be greatly appreciated.

The reputation of this business has been well known over the years, and it is our responsibility to continue to enhance it. Going forward, my husband and I will continue to fulfill the legacy left by Jacky and Milan, maintaining the standards that helped them succeed.
